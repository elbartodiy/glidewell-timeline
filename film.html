For half a century, fixing a tooth meant wax, plaster, and weeks of waiting. This is the story of how that changed — and of the people who made the best dentistry affordable enough to reach everyone.

What this is

An honest film about modern dentistry — told through the people who live it.

Clinicians, makers, educators and patients. Not a commercial — a real look at how the craft moved from the bench to scanners, milling and AI, and what that means for the person in the chair.

Why it matters

“The best dentistry in the world doesn’t help the person who can’t afford to walk in.”

At its heart the film is about access. We follow the half-century push to make great dentistry affordable — and the smiles it finally reached. That’s the thread that ties every story together.

Why your voice

We’re building this from real, first-hand voices.

We’re inviting a small circle of people whose work and experience tell this story honestly. We’d be glad to have yours on the record — in your own words, on your own terms. No company script, no sales pitch. Just the truth of your work.

What taking part looks like
  • A relaxed, on-camera conversation about your path and your work — nothing to memorize, no script. We guide it.
  • Around it, we quietly film a little of your everyday practice for texture.
  • We come to you, work around your schedule, and stay out of the way of your patients.
crew
A small, unobtrusive crew. Just two or three people with a camera and some lights — not a film set. Easy, calm, and over before you know it.
You’re in good company

The film brings together respected, independent voices across dentistry — clinicians, scientists, educators and the rising doctors of the Guiding Leaders community — building toward the celebration at Dentistry on the Rise this fall.
